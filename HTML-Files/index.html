Providing the Ultimate Social Media Security

SecureMySocial addresses the business, legal, regulatory, physical, and human risks of social media by warning people in real-time if they post problematic material so as to ensure immediate removal.

REAL TIME WARNINGS

PROVIDING THE ULTIMATE SOCIAL MEDIA SECURITY
A business activates a set of social media security rules (which can be customized). For example: Scan for various internal keywords, product line names, employee names, competitor names, project names, ticker symbols, or intellectual property terms. Regular expression and natural language analysis can also be used. The company sends a link to the employee with instructions to opt‐in and activate. Activation takes less than 10 seconds. SecureMySocial then informs employees using social media in real time if they (or others tagging them) are violating such rules.

EASY TO USE

INVITE-BASED SIGNUPS, CUSTOMIZABLE SECURITY RULES
With its robust, cloud‐based, human‐behavioral‐risk scanning engine, SecureMySocial warns users in realtime of posts, settings, and other social media activity that may expose them or their employers to realworld physical, financial, professional, and personal risk. Employers do not need to ask their employees for credentials – so businesses benefit from the safety they want and need while avoiding legal and human issues. The psychology‐based system helps organizations secure their employees, brands, and reputations, while also ensuring compliance with government rules and regulations, and offering a reasonable Due Care defense in case of any violations.
